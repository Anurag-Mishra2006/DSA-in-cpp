there are two types of traversal technique: 
        1. BFS : Breadth First Search (ek level wale ko pahle dekh lo)
        2. DFS : Depth First Search (different level)
                : Types: 
                    1. Inorder traversal (Left Root Right)
                    2. PreOrder traversal (Root Left Right)
                    3. PostOrder traversal (Left Right Root)
